Introduction

As education tech advances, new research has found that music is one of the most effective forms of teaching. Specific songs can invoke certain emotions depending on the song's genre, tempo, rhythm and lyrical elements. It has been shown in research that music has a grand impact on a person's mental space and reiterates the importance of the curation of music. Learning exploits the brain’s emotional responses to various types of music, establishing connections with specific neural pathways. This allows music to directly convey content through listening. As the user focuses on the tempo, sound elements, and lyrics that are able to understand the content more and more (Saarikallio, Suvi).

In the year 2123, technology has become so intricately advanced that society and artificial intelligence have come together, with humanity dependent on personal agents for fueling automation and learning. In one of its applications, AI and music have mended together to modernize everyday life to change the way younger generations are educated. IntellectCorps, the world’s leading machine learning corporation, created Chadwick AI with the purpose of revolutionizing individual learning plans, implementing personalized recommendations tailored to each student. Their program promises to create brighter minds by helping students make decisions through music recommendations that adapt to their individualized academic and personal profile.
