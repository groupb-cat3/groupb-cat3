New School Year!

As Mia is starting off her new school year, she is trying to decide on what elective courses that she wants to take. Her school offers different types of courses that everyone can do depending on their academic interests and prospective majors. She engages in a conversation with her AI as she does not exactly know what she was to do. Because Chadwick AI has been her academic assistant since middle school, he is programmed to know her strengths and interests, therefore becoming her best advisor when making decisions regarding her future.

***Unlike modern artificial intelligence programs, Chadwick AI was programmed by his creators to be of a male gender under the belief that by doing so, he would be able to assume a wiser character and a powerful, all-knowing demeanor (Ahn el al. 2022).***
